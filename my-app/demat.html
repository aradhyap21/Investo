Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Demat Account? +
A Demat (Dematerialized) Account is an account that holds your securities (shares, bonds, mutual funds) in electronic form, similar to how a bank account holds your money.
What documents are needed? +
You need PAN Card, Address Proof (Aadhaar/Passport/Driving License), Bank Account Details, and Passport-size photographs to open a Demat account.
What are the charges involved? +
Common charges include account opening fees, annual maintenance charges (AMC), transaction fees, and brokerage charges. These vary by broker and service provider.
