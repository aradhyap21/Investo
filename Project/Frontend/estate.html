Why Invest in REITs?

Diversification

Add real estate to your portfolio without the need to purchase physical properties. Spread your investment across multiple properties and sectors.

Regular Income

REITs are required to distribute at least 90% of their taxable income to shareholders annually, providing steady dividend payments.

Liquidity

Unlike physical real estate, REIT shares can be bought and sold easily on major exchanges, providing better liquidity for investors.

Inflation Hedge

Real estate has historically served as a hedge against inflation, with property values and rental income often increasing during inflationary periods.
